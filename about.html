Why Siteafi Exists

The same three problems,
over and over again.

01
The Price Was Out of Reach

Professional web agencies charge anywhere from thousands to tens of thousands of dollars for a website. For a small business owner already wearing a dozen hats, that number isn't just expensive — it's impossible. So they went without, and their competitors didn't.

02
They Didn't Know Where to Start

Even when business owners tried to figure it out themselves, the process felt overwhelming. What platform? What host? What domain? What does any of this cost? The lack of clear, honest information left them paralyzed — and made it easy for the wrong people to take advantage.

03

This is the one that stings. Because these business owners trusted the people they hired. They didn't know enough to push back when a simple 5-page site turned into a bloated $8,000 proposal packed with features they'd never use. They paid it. They felt burned. And they had every right to.

The Story

Where Siteafi
came from.

A veteran-owned small business, built from firsthand experience watching great businesses get bad deals.

The Beginning

Siteafi was born out of years spent building websites for small businesses across Texas — restaurants, salons, contractors, retailers. The work was good. The relationships were real. But the same story kept coming up, over and over again.

The Pattern

Business owners would come in frustrated. They'd already been to someone else — paid a lot of money — and ended up with a site they didn't understand, features they never used, and a monthly bill that never seemed to end. They weren't angry at websites. They were angry at being misled.

"I didn't know what I was buying, and nobody helped me understand."
The Breaking Point

There's a particular kind of frustration that comes from watching someone get taken advantage of — especially when they trusted the person doing it. Business owners were being sold bloated proposals packed with features they'd never use, at prices that were never justified. They felt taken advantage of. And they had every right to. As a small business owner ourselves, that hit close to home.

The Solution

Siteafi was built to close that gap. Premium templates, one-time pricing, free hosting, no unnecessary features, no confusing upsells. Everything a small business actually needs to get online — and nothing they don't. The kind of product we wished existed every time we had that conversation.

Yes — completely free. We build our templates for GitHub Pages and Cloudflare Pages, both of which offer free static site hosting. You pay nothing to keep your site live.
