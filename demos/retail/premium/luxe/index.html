Our Story

Objects chosen
with intention.

Luxe opened because we believed gifting deserved better. Not more options — better ones. We travel, vet, and personally select every piece in our collection. If we wouldn't give it to someone we love, it doesn't make the cut. That standard has guided every buying decision since we opened our doors.
