Our Story

Acquired with
intention.

Gilt was built on a simple principle — that the most meaningful possessions are the ones that took effort to find. We travel, research, and personally authenticate every piece in our collection. Nothing here is mass-produced. Everything has been chosen because it deserves to be owned by someone who will truly appreciate it.
