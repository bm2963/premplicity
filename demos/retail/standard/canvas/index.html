Our Story

A shop for people
who make things.

Canvas Supply Co. opened in 2020 as a small art supply store in a converted garage. We've grown since then, but the mission hasn't changed — stock the good stuff, teach people how to use it, and make creativity accessible to everyone.

🎨
Curated, Not Overwhelming
We stock what actually works. No shelf-fillers, no cheap substitutes.

Workshops for Every Level
