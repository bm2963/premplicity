What We Do

Our services.

01
Panel Upgrades

Breaker box replacements and load upgrades completed to current code. We document every panel we touch and pull all required permits.

02
EV Charger Installation

Level 2 home charger installation for all vehicle makes. Dedicated circuit, permit, and final inspection — done right the first time.

03
Whole-Home Rewire

Complete rewiring for older homes. We minimize disruption, match or improve your existing layout, and guarantee the work for 2 full years.

04
Lighting Design & Install

Recessed lighting, fixtures, ceiling fans, and dimmer systems. We handle layout, wiring, and installation — you just pick the fixtures.

05
Generator Installation

Standby and portable generator hookups with transfer switches. Keep your home powered through outages safely and automatically.

06
Emergency Electrical

Sparking panels, tripping breakers, exposed wiring, and power failures. We respond 24/7 and treat every emergency as our top priority.
