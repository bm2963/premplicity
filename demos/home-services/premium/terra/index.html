What We Do

Our services.

01
Landscape Design

Full design and installation of new landscape beds, planting schemes, and outdoor living spaces. We handle concept through completion.

02
Hardscaping

Patios, walkways, retaining walls, and raised beds. Natural stone, pavers, and concrete — built to last and designed to impress.

03
Outdoor Lighting

Low-voltage landscape lighting design and installation. Uplighting, path lighting, and accent fixtures that transform your yard at night.

04
Irrigation Design

Smart irrigation system design and installation. Zone-based drip and spray systems with smart controller integration and water usage optimization.

01
Weekly Lawn Care

Mowing, edging, blowing, and trimming on a consistent schedule. Assigned crew, consistent results, no reminders needed.

02
Shrub & Hedge Trimming

Shaped properly to promote healthy growth. Ornamental grasses, hedges, and foundation shrubs maintained to their natural form.

03
Fertilization Program

Soil-tested, seasonal fertilization plan. We match nutrients to your lawn's actual needs — not a generic schedule.

04
Irrigation Maintenance

Spring startup, fall shutdown, mid-season checks, and repair. We keep your irrigation system running at peak efficiency year-round.

01
Seasonal Cleanups

Spring and fall cleanups, leaf removal, and bed prep. We get your property looking sharp for the season and protect your plants through the off-season.

02
Mulch & Bed Installation

Premium mulch installation, bed edging, and weed barrier. Clean lines, proper depth, and the right material for each bed's conditions.

03
Tree & Shrub Planting

Specimen tree installation, privacy hedge planting, and ornamental bed expansions. We source quality stock and warranty our plantings.

04
Sod & Seeding

New lawn installation, bare spot repair, and overseeding. We match the right grass variety to your soil, sun, and irrigation conditions.
