What We Offer

Our services.

Cut & Style

A thorough consultation before scissors touch your hair. Precision cutting built around your face, lifestyle, and how you actually style at home. Never rushed.

From $95
Colour

Single process, highlights, and full colour by our senior colourists. Professional-grade only, with a deep commitment to hair health at every step.

From $145
Balayage

Hand-painted results that look genuinely effortless. Our most requested service and the one that requires the most skill to do beautifully. We do it beautifully.

From $235
Colour Correction

Restoring hair that's been damaged or incorrectly coloured elsewhere. We assess thoroughly, plan carefully, and execute over as many sessions as your hair actually needs.

Consultation required
Bridal & Events

Trial sessions and day-of styling for brides and their parties. We travel to your venue and take any party size. Begin consultations at least 10 weeks before your date.

From $290
Hair Rituals

Therapeutic treatments using Kerastase protocols — deep conditioning, bond restoration, and scalp care. Paired with a consultation and a bespoke at-home regimen.

From $90
