What We Do

Our services.

Cut & Style

A thorough consultation before scissors touch your hair. Precision cutting adapted to your face shape, lifestyle, and how you actually style your hair at home.

From $95
Color

Single process, highlights, full balayage, and corrective work by our senior colorists. We use only professional-grade color systems and take only as many clients as we can do properly.

From $145
Balayage

Hand-painted, sun-kissed results that look effortless because they are. Our most requested service — and the one most other salons get wrong. Low maintenance, long-lasting, and genuinely beautiful.

From $225
Keratin Treatment

Frizz elimination and smoothing that lasts 4–6 months. Reduces blow-dry time dramatically. Our formulas are free of formaldehyde and safe for color-treated hair.

From $350
Hair & Scalp Treatment

Therapeutic treatments using Kerastase protocols to address damage, dryness, and scalp health. Includes consultation, treatment, and at-home care recommendations.

From $85
Bridal Services

Trial sessions and day-of hair styling for brides and bridal parties. We travel to your venue and work with any party size. Book your trial at least 8 weeks in advance.

From $280
