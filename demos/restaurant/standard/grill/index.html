Our Way

Meat.
Fire.
Done.

We opened in 2012 with one rule — cook great meat over real fire and don't overthink it. We source USDA Prime, build fires with post oak and hickory, and let the process do the work. No shortcuts. No freezers. No microwave in sight.

Book a Table
01
USDA Prime Only

We don't use Choice or Select. Every steak is USDA Prime — the top 2% of American beef.

02
Real Wood Fire

Post oak and hickory. No gas assist. It takes longer and requires more skill. That's the point.

03
Everything Housemade

Every sauce, rub, pickle, and side made in-house from scratch. Nothing comes out of a bag or a can.

04
Walk-In Weekends

We seat walk-ins only on Friday and Saturday. First come, first served — the way it should be.
