Our Story

A place people
come back to.

Hearth opened in 2013 with a wood-fired oven, twelve seats, and a menu that changed every week based on what was good at the market. The room got bigger. The team grew. But the cooking stayed the same — honest, seasonal, made from scratch. We believe that the best meal you'll ever have is the one you share with people you love. We just set the table.
