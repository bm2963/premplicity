Notre Histoire

A corner table
waiting for you.

Bistro Margot opened as a love letter to the kind of French restaurant you stumble into on a side street in Paris — unpretentious, ingredient-driven, and run by people who genuinely care about the experience. Our menu changes with the seasons, our wine list changes with what excites us, and the room is always warm.
