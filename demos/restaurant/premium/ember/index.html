The Kitchen

Thirty years
of learning
fire.

Chef Marcus Holt spent a decade in kitchens across Lyon, San Sebastián, and Copenhagen before returning to Houston with a single conviction: that wood fire is the most honest cooking technique in the world. Ember is the restaurant he always wanted to cook in — intimate, seasonal, and completely uncompromising.

Marcus Holt
Executive Chef & Owner

The wine
list.

Over 180 labels across old and new world producers, curated by our sommelier with a focus on small producers, biodynamic farming, and the kind of wines that actually taste like somewhere. Our list changes constantly. Ask what's open.

Burgundy · France

Rioja · Spain
