Our Story

Rooted in
tradition,
reimagined.

Saveur was born from a deep reverence for Louisiana's culinary heritage — its bold flavors, its layered history, its soulful generosity. We take that foundation and push it forward, season by season.

Chef Marie Delacroix trained in Lyon before returning to her hometown of New Orleans, bringing with her a French precision that honors the Creole spirit rather than subduing it.

— Chef Marie Delacroix
