A decade

the perfect plate.

Chef Dante Reyes spent years cooking in Lyon, Madrid, and Copenhagen before returning to Houston to open Cellar. His philosophy is deceptively simple: pair extraordinary wine with food that deserves it. No gimmicks, no foam, no spectacle — just exceptional produce, honest technique, and the conviction that a great meal is its own occasion.

Dante Reyes
