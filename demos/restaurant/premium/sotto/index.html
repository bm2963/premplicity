The Story

Scratch kitchen.
Real people.

Sotto opened in 2008 with one griddle, two cooks, and a determination to serve the best breakfast in Houston. We haven't changed our buttermilk batter since. Everything that comes out of our kitchen is made from scratch, every morning, by a team that actually cares about the food on your plate.
