Grown with
intention.
Cooked with love.

We built Harvest around a single question: what if a restaurant's menu started at the farm, not the kitchen? Every dish we serve reflects what's in season this week, grown within 100 miles, by farmers we know by name. We change our menu constantly — because the land changes constantly, and that's exactly how it should be.

Where It Comes From

Our farm partners.

We visit every farm on this list. These aren't vendors — they're partners.

01
Lazy Creek Farm
Brenham, TX · 80mi

Eggs, seasonal vegetables, herbs, and edible flowers. Family-run for three generations.

02
Sunridge Orchards
Fredericksburg, TX · 140mi

Stone fruit, peaches, and apples. We build our seasonal compotes around whatever's ready.

03
Millstone Mill
Austin, TX · 160mi

Heirloom grain flours. Every loaf of our sourdough starts here.

04
Wild Branch Apiary
Navasota, TX · 60mi

Single-origin Texas honeys, rotating by season and hive location. Try them all.
