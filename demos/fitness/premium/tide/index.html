Practice is
not performance.
It's presence.

We built Tide because we believed most fitness studios had it backwards — designing for how people look instead of how they feel. Every class we offer, every instructor we hire, and every design decision in this space comes from one intention: to give you an hour that actually changes how you move through the rest of your day.
