Training Programs

Built for Every Runner

01
5K Foundation

8-week coached program for new runners or those returning after time off. Build your base, find your pace, cross your first finish line.

Beginner
02
10K Push

For runners with a solid base looking to go longer and faster. Speed work, tempo runs, and long run progression over 10 weeks.

Intermediate
03
Half Marathon

12-week half marathon training program with structured long runs, race pace work, and taper. Coach-guided start to finish.

Intermediate+
04
Marathon Training

The full 18-week marathon build. Periodized training, group long runs, and the accountability of a team going the same direction as you.

Advanced
05
Weekly Group Runs

Three coached group runs per week at multiple paces. No program commitment required — just show up and run with your people.

All Levels
06
Speed & Track

Tuesday night track workouts focused on speed development. Intervals, tempo repeats, and racing strategy for competitive runners.
