What We Do

Our services.

01
AC Installation

New central air installation including load calculation, equipment selection, and commissioning. All major brands — Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Rheem.

02
AC Repair

Comprehensive diagnostics and repair for all AC makes and models. We fix it right the first time — no parts-swapping guesswork.

03
Refrigerant Services

Refrigerant recharge, leak detection, and EPA-compliant handling. Certified for R-22, R-410A, and next-generation refrigerants.

04
Preventive Maintenance

Bi-annual tune-up program that extends system life and prevents breakdowns. Documented service history on every visit.

01
Furnace Installation & Repair

Gas and electric furnace installation and repair. Heat exchanger inspection, burner cleaning, and full system commissioning included.

02
Heat Pump Systems

Heat pump installation and service for year-round efficiency. Single-stage, two-stage, and variable speed systems available.

03
Emergency Heating Repair

24/7 emergency response for heating failures. Critical in the rare Houston cold snaps — we prioritize vulnerable households.

04
Gas Line Inspection

Safety inspection and testing of gas lines serving HVAC equipment. Leak detection, pressure testing, and code compliance verification.

01
Duct Cleaning

NADCA-standard duct cleaning using negative pressure and rotary brush systems. Removes dust, mold, and allergens from your air distribution.

02
Air Purification

Whole-home air purifiers, UV germicidal lights, and HEPA filtration. Hospital-grade air quality for homes with allergies or respiratory concerns.

03
Humidity Control

Whole-home humidifiers and dehumidifiers integrated with your HVAC. Optimal humidity year-round protects your home and your health.

04
Smart Thermostat Installation

Nest, Ecobee, and Honeywell smart thermostat installation with full app setup. Average energy savings of 15–23% on HVAC costs.
