We only stock
what we'd use
ourselves.

Grove started as a personal frustration. We couldn't find a shop that took ingredient sourcing seriously without making it feel clinical or exclusive. So we built one. Every product on our shelves has been tested, vetted, and approved by someone on our team. We'd rather carry 100 products we believe in than 1,000 that just fill space.

Our Story

Rooted in what
actually works.

Grove opened in 2019 with a single rule: if we wouldn't put it on our own skin or in our own bodies, it doesn't belong on our shelves. Every brand we carry is small-batch, ethically sourced, and genuinely effective — chosen through months of testing, not because of how the packaging looks.
