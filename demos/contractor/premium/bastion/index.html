What We Do

Our services.

01
Roof Replacement

Full tear-off and replacement for residential and commercial roofs. We work with asphalt shingle, metal, TPO, EPDM, and modified bitumen systems.

02
Roof Repair

Leak diagnosis and targeted repairs that address the actual source — not just the symptom. We provide a written warranty on every repair we complete.

03
Flat Roofing

Commercial flat roof installation and maintenance. TPO, EPDM, and modified bitumen systems installed by certified applicators with manufacturer warranties available.

04
Waterproofing

Below-grade and above-grade waterproofing for foundations, decks, balconies, and flat roofs. Includes drainage solutions and long-term moisture management.

01
Foundation Repair

Pier installation, crack repair, and stabilization for residential and light commercial foundations. Engineering consultation and permitted work on every project.

02
Structural Steel

Beam replacement, header installation, and structural steel repair. We work with licensed structural engineers to ensure every repair is designed and documented properly.

03
Storm Damage Restoration

Full structural assessment and restoration after wind, hail, and flood events. We work directly with insurance adjusters and document everything for your claim.

04
Masonry & Chimney

Tuckpointing, brick repair, chimney rebuilding, and masonry waterproofing. Restores structural integrity and prevents water infiltration at critical transition points.

01
24-Hour Emergency Response

Immediate dispatch for active leaks, storm damage, and structural emergencies. We arrive within hours — not days — to secure your property and prevent further loss.

02
Emergency Tarping

Rapid temporary weatherproofing after storm events. Properly secured tarping that protects your property until permanent repairs can be completed.

03
Insurance Claim Support

We've worked with every major insurer operating in Texas. We document damage thoroughly, communicate directly with adjusters, and advocate for a fair settlement.

04
Board-Up & Securing

Emergency board-up and structural securing for damaged openings, compromised walls, and unsafe structures after storm or impact events.
