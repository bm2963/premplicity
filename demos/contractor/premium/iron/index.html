What We Do

Our services.

01
Custom Metalwork

Bespoke steel, iron, and aluminum fabrication for architectural, commercial, and industrial applications. Designed to spec, built to outlast the structure around it.

02
Structural Steel

Structural steel fabrication and erection for commercial buildings, industrial facilities, and specialty structures. Engineered and certified in-house.

03
Architectural Elements

Staircases, railings, gates, facades, and custom architectural metalwork. Built to the designer's exact specification with the craftsmanship to match.

04
Industrial Fabrication

Heavy industrial fabrication for refineries, plants, and manufacturing facilities. ASME and AWS certified welders on every industrial project.

01
Commercial Build-Out

Full commercial construction from foundation to finish. We self-perform structural, mechanical, and specialty work that other contractors subcontract out.

02
Industrial Construction

Industrial facility construction and expansion. Process piping, equipment setting, and complex mechanical installations in operating facilities.

03
Design-Build

Single-source project delivery from concept through construction. Our in-house engineering team integrates design and build under one accountable contract.

04
Specialty Installations

Crane-assisted heavy lifts, precision equipment installation, and specialty rigging for complex mechanical and structural projects.

01
Historic Metalwork

Restoration and replication of historic iron and steel elements. We match original profiles, finishes, and joinery methods to preserve architectural authenticity.

02
Structural Repair

Structural steel repair, reinforcement, and replacement for aging commercial and industrial buildings. Non-destructive testing and full engineering documentation provided.

03
Coating & Finishing

Industrial coatings, galvanizing, powder coating, and specialty finishes applied in our own facility. SSPC certified surface preparation and application.

04
Maintenance Programs

Scheduled inspection and maintenance programs for steel structures, equipment supports, and architectural metalwork. Annual reporting and documented work history.
