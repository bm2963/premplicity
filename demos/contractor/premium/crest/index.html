What We Do

Our services.

01
Kitchen Remodeling

Full kitchen transformations from layout redesign to custom cabinetry, countertops, appliances, and finish work. We handle permits, plumbing, and electrical in-house.

02
Bathroom Renovation

Master suites, guest baths, and powder rooms. Tile work, custom showers, freestanding tubs, vanities, and heated floors. Done right, tiled to last.

03
Whole-Home Remodel

Complete interior renovations that transform your home while you're living in it. Phased scheduling, dedicated project manager, and weekly progress updates.

04
Flooring

Hardwood, tile, LVP, and carpet installation. We remove the old, prep the subfloor properly, and install to manufacturer specs with a written warranty.

01
Outdoor Living

Covered patios, pergolas, outdoor kitchens, and fire pit areas. We design and build spaces that extend your living area into the backyard.

02
Exterior Painting

Full exterior repaints including prep, primer, and two finish coats. We use premium exterior paint systems with a 5-year color retention guarantee.

03
Siding & Trim

Fiber cement, vinyl, and wood siding replacement. Includes soffit, fascia, and trim work for a complete, finished exterior appearance.

04
Windows & Doors

Window and door replacement with proper flashing, insulation, and finishing. Energy-efficient options available with utility rebate guidance.

01
Room Additions

Master suites, in-law suites, sunrooms, and bonus rooms seamlessly integrated with your existing structure. Full architectural and engineering coordination included.

02
Second Story

Full second-story additions that double your square footage without expanding your footprint. Structural engineering, permits, and inspections handled entirely in-house.

03
Garage Conversion

Convert your attached garage into a livable space — home office, gym, studio, or ADU. Includes insulation, HVAC, electrical, and finish work.

04
ADUs

Detached guest houses and backyard cottages that add property value and rental income potential. Design, permits, and construction all in one contract.

How It Works

Our process.

01
Free Consultation

We visit your home, understand your goals, and provide an honest scope-of-work and budget estimate. No pressure, no obligation.

02
Design & Planning

We develop your design, finalize material selections, pull permits, and create a detailed project schedule before construction begins.

03
Construction

A dedicated crew and project manager on your job every day. Weekly photo updates and a direct line to your PM throughout the build.

04
Final Walkthrough

A thorough punch list process and final walkthrough that doesn't close until every detail is exactly right. Backed by our 10-year warranty.
