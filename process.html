The Process

From browse
to live.

Five straightforward steps. No agencies, no developers, no monthly fees. Just your business — beautifully online.

A professional website from an agency can cost thousands of dollars and months of back-and-forth. Siteafi puts that same quality in your hands — for a fraction of the price.

01
Step One

Browse &
choose.

Head to the templates page and filter by your industry. Restaurant, salon, contractor, retail — we've built each template with your specific customers in mind. Click any card to see a live preview of exactly what your site will look like.

50+ Templates Live Previews Filter by Industry
02
Step Two

Purchase &
download.

One simple payment. No subscriptions, no recurring fees, no surprises. Once you check out, your template is delivered instantly to your inbox as a zip file — everything you need, ready to go.

One-Time Payment Instant Download No Subscriptions
03
Step Three

Customize
your site.

Open the HTML file in any text editor and swap in your business name, your photos, your contact info, and your story. No coding knowledge required — the files are clean, well-organized, and simple to edit. What you see is what you get.

No Code Required Clean Files Any Text Editor
04
Step Four

Deploy &
go live.

Upload your files to GitHub and connect to Cloudflare Pages or GitHub Pages. Completely free hosting — no server costs, no setup fees, nothing. Your site goes live on a real URL in minutes. Connect your own custom domain whenever you're ready.

Free Hosting Custom Domain Ready Live in Minutes
05
Step Five

Launch &
grow.

Your business is online. Share your link, put it on your business cards, add it to your social profiles. You own everything — the files, the site, the domain. No one can take it down, change the price on you, or hold your content hostage.

You Own It All No Lock-in Built to Last
